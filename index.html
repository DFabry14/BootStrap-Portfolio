About Me


Dan Fabry is a 30 year-old coding student from Chicago, Illinois. He was born and raised in Chicago and Park Ridge, IL, and attended Maine South High School, graduating in 2005. From there, he attended Long Beach State University, where he majored in Journalism and played for the 49er's Men's Volleyball Team until 2009.

After college, Dan spent a year coaching volleyball before going to Sweden to play professionally for two seasons for Tierp Volleyball Club. They finished tied for third in the league both years he played. After that, he returned to the US and coached for Northwestern University Women's Volleyball Team for the 2012 season. After that, he worked for a year before going back overseas to London to get his Master's Degree in Sport Management and playing for the University of East London volleyball team. After returning from London he found a job with Guaranteed Rate's insurance division, where he worked for two years in sales. He joined the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp in April of 2018.

He currently lives in Chicago's West Loop neighborhood with his girlfriend, Morgan, and their black lab, Norman.
